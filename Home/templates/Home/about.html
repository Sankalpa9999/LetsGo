About LetsGo

Transforming Transportation and Delivery Services

Our Mission

In this age of rapid technological progress, the way we move and deliver goods is undergoing a significant transformation. As our world becomes more interconnected, the demand for transportation and package delivery services that are not only suitable but also efficient and reliable has never been more evident.

“LetsGo” is not just a typical online platform; it's a complete solution crafted to meet the diverse needs of today's consumers and businesses.

Our History

Established in 2010 and situated in Newroad-5, Pokhara, “LetsGo” has been committed to offering a smooth online booking experience for a range of vehicles and delivery services. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include flexible car rental choices and streamlined package delivery services, making “LetsGo” a one-stop solution for all transportation and logistics needs.

Why Choose Us

  • Flexible car rental services
  • Reliable and efficient package delivery
  • User-friendly online platform
  • Responsive customer support
